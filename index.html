Who we are

JCP Cottage is a residential, non-medical care facility for the elderly. We bridge the gap between independent living and skilled nursing care. We also provide an environment in which residents can flourish in a small, protected community. While under our constant care and supervision, residents will enjoy a comfortable and healthy life.

24/7 Care and Supervision

Caregivers are always on site 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Secured Facility

This facility uses surveillance cameras to protect the safety of residents.

6 bedrooms

There are 6 private bedrooms to ensure the privacy and comfort of our residents.
